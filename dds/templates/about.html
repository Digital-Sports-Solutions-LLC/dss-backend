About Us

Our Mission

Welcome to Digital Sports Solutions LLC, where we are dedicated to revolutionizing the collegiate dodgeball experience through technology and a passion for innovation. Our mission is clear: to develop an exceptional app that enhances every aspect of the game, bringing together players, teams, and spectators in an unprecedented way. Collaborating closely with the National Collegiate Dodgeball Association (NCDA), we have harnessed their expertise and insights to create a solution tailored to the unique dynamics of collegiate dodgeball. Our app is not just a tool; it's a game-changer that aims to elevate the entire dodgeball community. Join us on this exciting journey as we redefine the future of collegiate dodgeball!
